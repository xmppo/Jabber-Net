OnProtocol

Modifiers: abstract

Notifies the client about every jabber packet. This is a union of the OnPresence, OnMessage, and OnIQ methods. Use this *or* the other 3 methods, but not both, as a matter of style.

OnStreamHeader

OnStreamInit
Signature: IEvent<StreamHandler,ElementStream>

Informs the client that a new stream has been inialized. You can add your packet factories to the new stream. NOTE: You may NOT make calls to the GUI in this callback, unless you call Invoke. Make sure you add your packet factories before calling Invoke, however.

OnWriteText
