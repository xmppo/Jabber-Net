Jabber-Net

ElementList

Namespace: JabberNet.jabber.protocol


Replacement for XmlElementList that removes the safety belt of checking for changes during traversal, but removes the big old memory leak in MS's implementation. Also, only returns first-level children, rather than all children below here with the given name. Thanks, MS.
