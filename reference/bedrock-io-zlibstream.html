ZlibStream

Namespace: bedrock.io


Wrap two ComponentAce.Compression.Libs.zlib.ZStream's, one in and one out. The existing wrappers in the project are uni-directional. No, System.IO.Compression.GZipStream won't work, because they didn't expose compression levels or flush types.
